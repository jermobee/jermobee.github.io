Engine Shampoo

 

Keeping a vehicle's engine clean actually will help it to run cooler, makes it easier to service and increases resale value. Engine compartment is shampooed with special degreasers & cleaners to break-down caked up grease and grime that has deposited on almost everything under the hood. Engine compartment is then dressed with a non silicone low sheen dressing.
